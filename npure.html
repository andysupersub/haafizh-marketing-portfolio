02 What I Did

My Contribution

Full creative pre-production — from writing the script to delivering a deck ready to present to the brand.

✍️
Script Writing
Wrote the creative script for the 30-second video — scene-by-scene, aligned to the key messages and brand voice of N'Pure.
🔍
Reference Research
Sourced visual references from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Pinterest — focused on product cinematography that feels organic and clean.
🗂️
Eagle Curation
Collected and tagged all references in Eagle by scene, product, and shot type — creating a visual library the production team could access at any time.
🎬
Timeline Moodboard
Arranged references into a scene-by-scene moodboard — wide shot, close-up, flying motion, overhead — following the script sequence.
🎨
PPT Deck Design
Designed the Creative Deck in PowerPoint to be visual, brand-aligned, and polished enough to present directly to N'Pure on the day.
