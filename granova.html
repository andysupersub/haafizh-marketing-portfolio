02 What I Did

My Contribution

End-to-end creative pre-production for a collab launch campaign — from the first script draft to a polished deck the client could take straight to the shoot.

✍️
Script Writing
Wrote the creative campaign script for the Kenangan Mantan Coffee Bar launch video — covering scene flow, voice over, and key messaging that highlighted the product's identity as a coffee-meets-granola collab snack.
🔍
Reference Research
Sourced visual references from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Pinterest — focusing on food product cinematography, lifestyle snack campaigns, café aesthetic, and collab brand tone that matched both Granova and Kopi Kenangan's identity.
🗂️
Curation in Eagle
Organized all visual references in Eagle by scene, shot type, and mood — creating a structured library the production team and director could navigate quickly on production day.
🎬
Storyboard & Moodboard
Built a full storyboard scene-by-scene — shot type, camera movement, focal length, talent direction, and props — plus a visual moodboard aligned to the warm, coffee-inspired campaign aesthetic.
🎨
PPT Deck Design
Designed the final Creative Deck in PowerPoint — visual, on-brand, and presentation-ready, covering the creative brief, script, storyboard, moodboard, and talent direction all in one polished document.

A full creative deck delivered for Granova's collab launch — covering everything from script to storyboard in a single polished presentation.
