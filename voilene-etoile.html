01 The Project

Instagram Feeds for a
Women's Fashion Brand

Voilène Etoile is a women's fashion brand designed to spread positive aura and bring new colors to every woman's body. The brand philosophy: comfort, beauty, and empowerment — for women of all sizes. I handled the visual identity and Instagram feeds design to help the brand launch with a cohesive, elegant aesthetic.

02 What I Did

My Scope of Work

Seluruh visual Instagram Voilène Etoile — dari moodboard hingga photo retouch — aku kerjakan untuk membangun brand image yang elegant, soft, dan konsisten.

01
Instagram Moodboard
Membangun visual direction Voilène Etoile di Instagram — palet warna lembut (krem, sage, teal), tipografi serif elegan, dan grid aesthetic yang mencerminkan filosofi brand: comfort and beauty for every body.
Graphic Design
02
Feeds Instagram Design
Mendesain seluruh konten feeds Instagram — product launch announcement, product collection posts (LAUNCHED, ADELINE, ANNELISE, BLANCHE, BLISSE, BIJOU) — dengan overlay tipografi yang konsisten dan on-brand di setiap post.
Graphic Design
03
Photo Retouch
Melakukan retouching pada seluruh product photography — skin correction, color grading yang konsisten, dan crop framing untuk memastikan setiap foto tampil cohesive dan berkualitas tinggi di feed Instagram.
Graphic Design

Selected Output
